# Udemy Courses Dashboard – Power BI

An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing Udemy courses data across subjects, levels, pricing, and subscriber engagement.

---

## Dashboard Pages

### Page 1 – Overview
- Avg Price: $66 | Total Lectures: 147.5K | Max Price: $200
- Total Subscribers: 11.8M | Total Courses: 2.49bn | Total Reviews: 575K
- Paid vs Free courses distribution
- Engagement rate by subject (Web Development leads at 5.39%)
- Avg subscribers by paid/free
- Total lectures by level
- Total subscribers by subject

### Page 2 – Deep Dive
- Top 10 courses by subscribers (Learn HTML5 Programming leads at 269K)
- Lectures by level and subject
- Subscribers by subject and level
- Avg price by subject and level

---

## Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Data Modeling
